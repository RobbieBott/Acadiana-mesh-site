Clock Out of Sync

Action Important

These repeaters have a clock offset greater than 5 minutes. A badly drifted clock can cause packet deduplication failures and timestamp confusion across the mesh. To fix, connect to the repeater via the MeshCore app or CLI and sync the clock.

How to fix: Connect to your repeater via the MeshCore companion app and tap Sync Clock, or via CLI run: set time [unix timestamp]. After syncing, the repeater should disappear from this list within one advert cycle (~10 minutes).
